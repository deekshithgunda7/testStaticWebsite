Email Phishing

  • Don't Click the link
  • Use 2-factor authentication
  • Delete recorded conversations
  • Keep It Clean
  • Be less Social
  • Don't sync for sync's sake
  • Keep off the beaten track
  • Let sleeping Bluetooth lie
  • Encrypt stored data
  • Patch your devices

What to do:

Don't click links in emails.Instead,type the URL you want directly into the browser

Why:

According to Microsoft,phishing is still the number one faverite method of cyber attacks

What to do:

Use a second factor for logging into accounts

Why:

If you have a robust two or muloti-factor in place,you are much less likely to lose personal data due to phishing

What to do:

Regularly delete any recorded conversations used by your personal assistant

Why:

There have been cases where Alexa revealed personal data to unknown persons without consent

What to do:

Make sure you keep data replication to a minimum.Delete old files you don't use

Why:

There can never be 100% security but reducing thre places that can be compramised helps lessen your risk

What to do:

Minimize the amount of personal data you have on social media platform

Why:

Information like your pet's name is sometimes used to recover account logins.Don't give hackers an easy way into your online accounts

What to do:

Disable automatic file and media sharing whenever posssible

Why:

A lot of devices set up cloud syncing when you first configure the device.Checkif you really want to store these data in the cloud

What to do:

Disable location tracking on each app

Why:

A recent study of almost 1 million Android phoes demonstrated that apps regularly harvested tracking data

What to do:

If you are not using Bluetooth,switch it off

Why:

Bluetooth vulnerabilties can allow data to be siphoned off your device

What to do:

Encrypt any data you store on hard drives and use an email encryption tool if you share personal data

Why:

Encryption is a layer of protection that can prevent lost or stolen data from being exposed

What to do:

Keep your computers and mobile devices patched and up to date

Why:

Software vulnerabilites allow malware to infect your device,which can steal data and login credentials
